About EasyOrder

Get to know who we are and the team behind your favourite fast food ordering website.

EasyOrder is an online fast food ordering website that lets you enjoy your favourite meals without the wait. We started with one simple goal: to make ordering food as quick and easy as a few clicks. From crispy fried chicken and juicy burgers to refreshing drinks and sweet desserts, our full menu is just a tap away.

Whether you are craving a quick lunch, feeding the whole family, or grabbing a late-night snack, EasyOrder brings hot and fresh meals straight to your doorstep. No more waiting in long queues or being put on hold over the phone. Simply browse the menu, add your items to the cart, and check out in minutes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver a fast, friendly and reliable food ordering experience for everyone. We are committed to serving freshly cooked meals at affordable prices, while making sure every order reaches you quickly and exactly the way you like it.

  • To make online food ordering simple and stress-free.
  • To serve freshly cooked, good quality meals every single time.
  • To offer affordable prices and great daily deals.
  • To deliver every order quickly and safely to your door.
